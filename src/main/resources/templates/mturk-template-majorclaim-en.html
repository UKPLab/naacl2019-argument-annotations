Guidelines:

  • The overall opinion is usually a short, clear statement about the product as a whole, such as "I am completely satisfied with this product" or "this product is disappointing and not worth the money".
  • The overall opinion should not refer only to specific parts or functions of the product, such as a phone's camera or a particular function of a multi-cooker.
  • Try to find a phrase or sentence that answers the question, "How good is this product?"
