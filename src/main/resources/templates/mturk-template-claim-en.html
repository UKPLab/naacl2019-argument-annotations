Read the entire product review below and note the author's overall opinion, which is highlighted within the review text. Then, try to identify individual claims (phrases or sentences) in the review that directly support or attack the author's overall opinion.

For example, a review for a hoverboard might read, "I think this hoverboard is great, since it is fun to ride. However, I should note that it was difficult to learn to use it," with "this hoverboard is great" as the author's overall opinion. An example of a supporting claim might be "it is fun to ride", and an example of an attacking claim might be "it was difficult to learn to use it".

For each supporting claim you find, press the "+because" button under the radio button marked (A), and a green text field will appear. Then use your pointer to highlight the supporting claim in the text. The highlighted text will be copied into the text field.

For each attacking claim you find, press the "+but" button under the radio button marked (A), and a red text field will appear. Then use your pointer to highlight the attacking claim in the text. The highlighted text will be copied into the text field.

If you make a mistake, you can replace the contents of the text field by highlighting again. You can also remove a text field by pressing the "x" button next to it.

If you cannot identify any supporting or attacking claims at all, then select the radio button marked (B) and type in, in your own words, a paraphrase of the author's overall opinion.

Guidelines:

  • The claims you highlight cannot overlap with the author's overall opinion.
  • Do not simply highlight all the positive and negative things the author says about the product. Focus only on the claims that directly support or attack the author's overall opinion.
  • Claims often begin with words such as "because", "since", "so", "but", and "however". However, you should omit these words when highlighting the claim.

After entering all the claims (or your paraphrase of the overall opinion), press the Submit button.
